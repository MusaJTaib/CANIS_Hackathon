Readability Score

Readability score is a measure of how easy it is to read and understand a written text. It's commonly used to assess the level of readability of a piece of writing, such as an article, blog post, or academic paper. The higher the easier it is to read.

Sentiment Analysis

Sentiment score is a numerical score assigned to a piece of text that indicates the overall sentiment expressed in the text.
Polarity score is a numerical score assigned to a text that indicates the degree of positive or negative sentiment expressed in the text. Polarity score ranges from -1 to +1, where -1 indicates highly negative sentiment and +1 indicates highly positive sentiment, while a score of 0 indicates neutral sentiment.
Subjectivity score is a numerical score assigned to a piece of text that indicates the degree to which the text expresses a subjective opinion or a factual statement. Subjectivity score ranges from 0 to 1, where 0 indicates a completely objective text, and 1 indicates a completely subjective text.
